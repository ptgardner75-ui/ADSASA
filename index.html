The Mission

ADSASA is the definitive authority for Audio Description in South Africa[cite: 1]. We bridge the gap between world-class filmmaking and the 1.4 million visually impaired citizens currently excluded from our national narrative[cite: 1].

Compliance & Services

Regulatory Auditing Protecting broadcasters from R5 Million ICASA non-compliance penalties through rigorous certification[cite: 1].
NADS Certification The "Gold Standard" in Audio Description, ensuring every frame is translated into verbal art[cite: 1].
Community Verification Direct scripting and review by blind professionals—ensuring authenticity, not just automated noise[cite: 1].
Strategic Implementation End-to-end guidance for streamers and production houses to meet global accessibility benchmarks[cite: 1].
