Who We Are

ADSASA is an independent global standards and accreditation body for Audio Description, founded in South Africa with a vision that transcends borders.

We set the benchmark for excellence in accessible storytelling across film, television, streaming, and live performance.

Our Mandate

"Nothing About Us Without Us"

We believe accessibility is a human right. Every standard we create is shaped, reviewed, and verified by blind and visually impaired audiences.

What We Do

  • Define world-class Audio Description standards
  • Certify production companies and media organisations
  • Audit and elevate accessibility quality
  • Support creators and broadcasters globally
  • Champion inclusive storytelling

Standards Framework

Our rigorous framework covers editorial excellence, technical precision, community verification, and both live and automated systems.

Global Vision

Born in South Africa. Built for the world. ADSASA delivers a universal standard that respects cultural context while maintaining uncompromising quality.
