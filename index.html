Who We Are

ADSASA is an independent standards and accreditation body for Audio Description (AD), originating in South Africa and built for global adoption.

We define, elevate, and certify high-quality Audio Description practices across broadcast, streaming, film, cinema, advertising, and live events.

Our Mandate

"Nothing About Us Without Us"

Accessibility is a human right. No Audio Description is considered compliant unless it has been shaped, reviewed, and verified by blind and visually impaired users.

What We Do

  • Define international Audio Description standards
  • Certify compliant AD production and delivery
  • Audit and assure quality in media accessibility
  • Support broadcasters, streamers, and content creators
  • Promote inclusive media practices worldwide

Standards Framework

Our structured compliance system covers:

  • Editorial and scripting excellence
  • Technical audio delivery specifications
  • Community verification processes
  • Live and automated Audio Description systems

Global Vision

Developed in South Africa, ADSASA is designed as a globally applicable framework that aligns with international accessibility best practices while remaining adaptable to different markets and languages.
