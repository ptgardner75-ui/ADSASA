NOTHING ABOUT US WITHOUT US

Who We Are

ADSASA is the national accrediting body for Audio Description in South Africa[cite: 1]. We bridge the gap between the film industry and the 1.4 million visually impaired citizens currently excluded from local content[cite: 1].

What We Do

We transform a "regulatory burden" into a tool for inclusion. We ensure broadcasters and streamers meet the R5 Million ICASA compliance wall through rigorous, community-verified standards[cite: 1].

  • Compliance Auditing & Risk Assessment
  • End-to-End Audio Description Production
  • The National Audio Description Standard (NADS) Certification
  • Inclusive Job Creation for the Blind Community

The Standard

No film, series, or broadcast may claim "compliance" unless their AD tracks are produced or audited by an ADSASA-Approved Service Provider[cite: 1]. Our seal ensures that visual cues are translated into verbal art with precision[cite: 1].
