ADSASA

Audio Description Standards Accreditation and Services Authority
“Nothing About Us Without Us”
ADSASA is the definitive authority for Audio Description in South Africa. We operate globally, bridging the gap between world-class filmmaking and the 1.4 million visually impaired citizens currently excluded from our national narrative.
Regulatory Auditing Independent compliance verification for broadcasters and streaming platforms.
NADS Certification Official accreditation under the National Audio Description Standard.
Community Verification Authentic review and scripting by blind and visually impaired professionals.
Strategic Implementation Guidance on accessibility workflow for global production houses.
